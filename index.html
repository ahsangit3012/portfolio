ABOUT ME

I’m a front-end developer and UX/UI designer passionate about crafting sleek, user-first interfaces that seamlessly merge design and function.

My work lives where pixel-perfect visuals, intuitive interaction, and story-driven content meet blending the precision of development with the creativity of photography and videography to create digital experiences people genuinely enjoy.

I’ve had the opportunity to intern twice at a media company, where I sharpened my creative and technical skills in a real-world environment. Alongside that, I’ve gained hands-on experience in customer service and crowd control through on-site roles helping me develop strong communication, adaptability, and people skills.

I’ve completed my Bachelor’s degree with a 2:1 division and continue to grow my passion for crafting interactive and user-friendly interfaces. Designing smooth, intuitive experiences excites me especially when it brings ideas to life through thoughtful front-end development.

Outside of academics, I dive into content creation. Photography and videography are more than just hobbies for me they’re creative outlets where I get to tell stories visually and connect with others through social media. And when I’m not behind a camera or screen, you’ll probably find me lost in the pages of a good novel.
